How it works

Request

When restaurants, food retailers, caterers, and individuals have a donation, they request a pickup from our Food Connect app. They will receive a confirmation with an ETA of the pickup and a link to track the driver's arrival in real-time.

Pick up

The Food Connect dispatch team will find the right match for your donation- based on food needs, location, and availability.

Deliver

Food Connect will deliver the donation to those in need, helping you make a bigger and better impact on the community.

What can you donate?

We accept every type of meal except from homemade food, here is a list of examples of possible donations:

Whole, uncut, and unpeeled fruits and vegetables
Dry, shelf-stable food in their original packaging
Cold food that has been stored below 41ºF
Prepared hot food stored above 135ºF (not homemade)
Unopened packaged/ canned/boxed food that isn’t damaged
Food that has not been on the serving line
Food made in a commercial kitchen by licensed vendors
Food that is not damaged or dented
